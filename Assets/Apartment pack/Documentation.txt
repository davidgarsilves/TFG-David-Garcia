1.Living room assets : fa , center table , television.
2.Bedroom assets : Bed , side tables.
3.Bathroom assets : Bath tub , washbasin , taps, shower curtains ,mirror.
4.Toilet assets : Toilet seat , toilet roll , toilet roll holder , dustbin.
5.Dining assets : Dining table , chair.
6. Kitchen assets : 6 different type of cabinets , stove , oven , dishwasher , sink , taps.
7.Structural assets :Structure1,Structure2,curtains , windows and  glass.

1.Whole Scene has 90K tris.
